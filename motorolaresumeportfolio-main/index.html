About Me

I am a Learning Specialist and Instructional Designer with a Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science and an upcoming Master of Education in Learning & Technology. My background uniquely bridges technical development, learning science, and business outcomes — enabling me to design data-informed, performance-focused learning experiences.

Across corporate training, higher education, and digital learning environments, I conduct performance needs assessments, learner analysis, and instructional design using models such as ADDIE and SAM. I specialize in building interactive elearning with Articulate Rise and Storyline, integrating learning analytics, and leveraging generative AI to streamline design workflows while maintaining high instructional quality.

My approach emphasizes intentional visual design, adult learning theory, scenario-based learning, and measurable outcomes — ensuring learning experiences are not only engaging but directly aligned to organizational goals.
